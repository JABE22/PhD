# Manual Details To Fill For POAI Submission

These are the details I cannot reliably infer from the workspace and you need to provide or verify manually.

## Author personal details

1. Full official Russian form of your name, if different from `Матармаа Ярно`.
2. Current position / job title.
3. Academic degree.
4. Academic rank.
5. Full postal address of your organization.
6. Home address.
7. Mobile / office phone number.
8. Fax, if required or available.
9. ORCID.
10. ResearcherID / Scopus Author ID / SPIN / AuthorID, if you want them included.
11. Exact research interests wording for the author-information block.

## Organization details

1. Official organization letterhead for the covering letter.
2. Name and position of the person authorized to sign the covering letter.
3. Signature and official stamp / seal, if used.
4. Full postal address of the organization exactly as your institution wants it written.

## External review details

1. Reviewer full name.
2. Reviewer academic degree and academic rank.
3. Reviewer position.
4. Reviewer organization.
5. Reviewer contact details.
6. Signature date.
7. Official seal / stamp of the detached organization.

## License agreement details

1. Exact author name format to use in the agreement.
2. Postal address and passport / ID details, if the agreement form requests them.
3. Signature date.
4. Whether the journal wants the agreement at submission or after acceptance.

## Manuscript details to verify manually

1. Final UDC index. `004.8` is a plausible placeholder but should be checked.
2. Final Russian title wording.
3. Final English title wording.
4. Whether Ukrainian metadata is still strictly required in the current 2026 workflow or only present in older templates.
5. Exact final page count after insertion into the official Word template.
6. Exact figure and table placement in the Word template.
7. Equation formatting in Microsoft Equation Editor / Word equation format as required by the journal.

## Bibliography details to verify manually

1. Which added PAI references are genuinely relevant enough to cite in the article text.
2. Which older references can be removed without weakening the argument.
3. Final citation order after manuscript revision, because POAI wants references in order of appearance.
4. Final GOST-style formatting in the Word manuscript, if the bibliography is reformatted manually.

## Submission workflow details to verify manually

1. Email address or submission contact to which the package should be sent.
2. Whether they accept only email or also require physical delivery.
3. Whether printed signed copies are still mandatory in practice for 2026 submissions.
4. Whether any additional forms are requested after editorial screening.
