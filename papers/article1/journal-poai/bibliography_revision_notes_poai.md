## Important limitation

Adding references to the `.bib` file is not enough by itself. They must also be cited in the article text where they are genuinely relevant. Otherwise they will not appear in the final reference list.

## Suggested citation placement

1. In the related-work section when discussing ontology and semantic representation:
   cite `nicenko2024ontology`.
2. In methods or implementation discussion when talking about datasets, semantic pipelines, or AI system construction:
   cite `pikalyov2024dataset`, `ustenko2024annotation`, `yermolenko2024deeplearning`.
3. In discussion of expert systems, symbolic or hybrid reasoning, and AI decision support:
   cite `mashchenko2024mivar`, `podoprigorova2024consensus`, `torzhkov2024ethical`, `shen2024semantic`.
